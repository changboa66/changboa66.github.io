# BlueSpider Privacy Policy
**Effective Date: May 8, 2026**

BlueSpider ("we," "us," or "our") understands the importance of privacy to you and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how the BlueSpider mobile application handles your information.

### 1. Data Collection and Use
BlueSpider is a local network debugging tool.
- **Local Processing**: All network traffic capture, decryption, and analysis are performed **locally on your device**.
- **No Data Upload**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your captured traffic data, personal information, or browsing history to any external servers.

### 2. Permissions
- **VPN Configuration**: BlueSpider requires VPN permissions to establish a local tunnel for traffic interception. This tunnel operates entirely locally and does not pass through any remote servers.
- **Root Certificate**: To decrypt HTTPS traffic, users must manually install and trust a local root certificate. This certificate is uniquely generated on your device and is used solely for local debugging.

### 3. Third-Party Services
To support the ongoing development of the application, we integrate the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob**: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use your advertising identifier (such as IDFA) to deliver personalized ads, manage ad frequency, and prevent fraud.
- **Data Tracking**: If you grant authorization via the iOS "Allow Tracking" prompt, we will use your IDFA to provide a more relevant advertising experience. You can withdraw this authorization at any time in the iOS system settings.

### 4. Commercialization and Ad Removal
We offer In-App Purchase (IAP) functionality to permanently remove all advertisements. Your purchase status will be associated with your Apple ID.

### 5. Policy Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend that you check this page periodically for any changes.

# Privacy Policy
**Last updated: June 4, 2026**

LetsQR ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect
**We do not collect any personal data.** LetsQR is designed to be fully offline and privacy-first. All data you enter — text, URLs, WiFi credentials, contact details, and images — is processed entirely on your device and never sent to any server.

## QR Code Generation
QR codes are generated locally using Core Image and Core Graphics. No input data is transmitted over the network. The app requires no internet connection to function.

## Photo Library Access
If you choose to save a QR code to your Photos, LetsQR will request write-only access to your photo library. This is used solely for saving images you explicitly request. We do not read, scan, or upload any photos from your library.

## iCloud / Sync
LetsQR does not use iCloud sync or any cloud services. All data is stored exclusively on your device using SwiftData.

## Third-Party Services
LetsQR does **not** include any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. There are no network requests made by the app other than those initiated by the system (e.g., opening a URL from a scanned QR code).

## Data Storage and Security
- QR code history and style templates are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
- We do not operate any servers, databases, or backend infrastructure.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

## Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:
- **Right to know** what data is collected — We collect nothing.
- **Right to deletion** — Delete the app to remove all locally stored data.
- **Right to opt out** — There is no data collection to opt out of.

## Children's Privacy
LetsQR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
